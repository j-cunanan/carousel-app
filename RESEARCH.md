# Carousel App — Competitive Research

- **Followers:** 9M
- **Posts:** 5,692
- **Contact:** Partnerships@Technology.io
- **Verified:** Yes

### Content Format
- **Multi-story carousel roundup** — each slide covers one tech story
- **Dark-themed slides** with blurred background photo + text overlay
- **Headline:** Bold, light-blue + white sans-serif (e.g., "A FREE APP JUST GUTTED THE REAL ESTATE PLAYBOOK")
- **Body:** 1-2 sentences explaining the story
- **Sources cited** at bottom (Reuters, Boston Dynamics, Anthropic, ByteDance, Replit, Google Chrome, Genesis AI, Stack Overflow)
- **Hashtags:** #ai #technology #robotics #innovation #future
- **Engagement:** 85.7K likes per post (typical)
- **NO tweet screenshots** used — all original graphics

### Typical Caption Content (from analyzed post)
- Multi-topic summary covering: robot monks, humanoid gymnasts, ByteDance AI animation tools, Boston Dynamics, Anthropic Claude, vibe coding tools

### Business Model
- Brand sponsorships as primary monetization
- Small team (~5 people), no external agencies
- AI-generated content for scale (likely Claude/ChatGPT + AI image gen)

### What @technology does NOT do
- Tweet screenshots or X/Twitter proof slides
- AI-generated cover art
- Video slides embedded in carousels
